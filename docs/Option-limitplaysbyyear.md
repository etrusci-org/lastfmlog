# [LastfmLog](../README.md) - Option: `--limitplaysbyyear`

`--limitplaysbyyear NUMBER`

Applies to action: `stats`

Limit the number of items in plays by year.


---


## Example

```text
cli.py stats --limitplaysbyyear 10
```
