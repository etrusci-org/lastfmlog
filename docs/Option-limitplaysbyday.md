# [LastfmLog](../README.md) - Option: `--limitplaysbyday`

`--limitplaysbyday NUMBER`

Applies to action: `stats`

Limit the number of items in plays by day.


---


## Example

```text
cli.py stats --limitplaysbyday 10
```
