# [LastfmLog](../README.md) - Option: `--limitplaysbymonth`

`--limitplaysbymonth NUMBER`

Applies to action: `stats`

Limit the number of items in plays by month.


---


## Example

```text
cli.py stats --limitplaysbymonth 10
```
